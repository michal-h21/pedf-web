Price list of fees and paid services

Overdue fees:

  • Overdue outside loan 3 Kč per 1 day

  • Overdue in-house loan 50 Kč per 1 day

  • Overdue e-book reader 100 Kč per 1 day

Overdue notices

  • Certified letter – according to prices of the Czech Post + processing 15 Kč

  • 4th notice sent by certified letter 250 Kč

  • Reminder before court action – depends on the fee of the lawyer

  • Before court action – 200 Kč/1 document

Compensation of a loss or damage of a document

  • Compensation of a loss or damage of a document - library document processing 50 Kč

  • Black and white copy of the compensating document 1 page/2 Kč

  • Ring-binding of the compensating document 250 Kč

  • Loss or damage of an electronic supply may be up to the original price of the supply

  • Compensation will be suggested by the library individually

  • Loss or damage of the barcode in a document 20 Kč

Interlibrary loan services

  • National interlibrary loans – free at libraries placed in Prague, depends on price list of the borrowing institution + postage

  • Copies from Czech libraries - depends on price list of the borrowing institution

  • International interlibrary loans - depends on individually price list of the borrowing institution and the postage

  • Copies from libraries abroad - depends on individuall price list of the borrowing institution and the postage
