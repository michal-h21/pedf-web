--- title: "Price list of fees and paid services" ---

Determination of Fees Paid by Students of the Faculty of Education, Charles University

Dean's Measure No. 9/2023

Ref. No.: UKPedF/270584/2023

Prepared by: Head of the Study Department, Library and IT Center

Responsible: Dean of the Faculty

Pursuant to the Rector's Directive of Charles University regarding the collection of fees for services provided to students, and based on a cost calculation, I hereby set the following fees for students of the Faculty of Education, Charles University:

B. Fees and Paid Services in the Faculty Library

Items B1–B4 are governed by Rector’s Directive OR 19/2022, Annex No. 4.

  1. Loss or damage of a barcode or RFID chip in a publication: CZK 20
  2. Late return fine according to the Unified Loan Matrix:
    • For home loans: CZK 3 per item per day
    • For in-library loans: CZK 50 per item per day
    • For both types of loans of e-book readers: CZK 100 per item per day
  3. Reminder fees:
    • 1st, 2nd, 3rd reminder by email: free of charge
    • Printed reminder by post: postage + CZK 15 administration fee
    • 4th reminder by registered mail: CZK 250 (administrative cost)
    • Pre-lawsuit reminder: according to the fee for 1 legal service act under §7 of the Attorney Tariff
    • Pre-lawsuit preparation: CZK 200 per document
  4. Compensation for lost/damaged library items:
    • Processing fee: CZK 50 per document
    • B/W copy of a page: CZK 2
    • Binding of replacement: CZK 250
    • Loss or damage of borrowed electronic devices: up to full purchase price
  5. Reprographic services:
    • B/W printing/copying:
      • A4 single-sided: CZK 2
      • A4 double-sided: CZK 3
      • A3 single-sided: CZK 3
      • A3 double-sided: CZK 5
    • Color printing/copying:
      • A4 single-sided: CZK 10
      • A4 double-sided: CZK 15
      • A3 single-sided: CZK 15
      • A3 double-sided: CZK 20
  6. Data media: A4 paper: CZK 1
  7. Research services:
    • Teachers and staff: free of charge
    • Other users: CZK 100 (administrative fee, even for no results)
    • Researcher’s work: CZK 250 per hour
  8. Spiral binding:
    • Self-service binding (per started 100 sheets): CZK 10
    • Order-based binding (per started 100 sheets): CZK 60
    • Cover (cardboard/transparent): CZK 10

Effective from: October 1, 2023

Issued by: Prof. PaedDr. Michal Nedělka, Dr., Dean
