Loan rules of the Library of Faculty of Education, Charles University

Full version of the Loan rules is available at the Circulation Desk, in the Study room and on the Library website.

Provided Services

The LFE provides its users the following services in particular:

  1. in-house and outside loan of documents: information resources in written, visual, audio, audio-visual, electronic or digital form; inter-library loan and international inter-library loan

  2. access to electronic information databases and other resources accessible on the Internet;

  3. other information services: reference and consulting services, information retrieval, scientific-informational, specific, counselling, promotional, and other services

  4. reprographic service – users can make copies on a copy machine in the Study room. These copies can be used only for their personal purposes, in accordance with the Copyright Act.

Basic services are out of charge, others follow the Price List of fees and paid services.

Users

Any adult person which owns a valid Charles University identification Card can start to use the services of the LFE. In order to become registered user, the person must sign the “Agreement of automatized library system loan“. During his visit user is obliged to present his CU Card to use library services of the LFE. CU Card is non delegable and user is due to notify the Library of its loss.

The Access level to documents and services is specified in the Uniform Borrowing Matrix, see at the Circulation Desk.

Every member of the University finishing his contract or his studies has a duty to prove no commitments to the Library.

Loan Conditions

The user is able to borrow a document only in case he doesn´t have any undischarged commitments to the Library.

The user is responsible for the documents he borrowed till the moment he hands it back and he is not permitted to borrow them to any other person. Already while borrowing the documents user should check the state of the documents, otherwise all damage will be on his charge.

Maximum possible number of documents to loan, time limits and reservations are described in the Uniform Borrowing Matrix.

Books purchased from special grants and funding are due to be processed by the Library. After that they are available to be borrowed for time necessary for research or teaching.

In case a document is not available in the Library, there is a possibility to ask for an inter-library loan.

E-sources are to be used according to their licences and to the Copyright Act. They are to be used for personal usage only and just for research or educational purposes, not for any commercial purposes, any further distribution or remote storing. Additional information is available at the Service Desk of the Study Room. Copying of audio or visual documents is forbidden.

In case of any violation of this Loan Rules, rights of the user can be blocked or cancelled. Any damage or theft of a document from the Library may result in legal steps against the user.

Users are allowed to administrate their library account via the Internet (reservation, prolongation etc.) as well as to check/print state of their account. In case of any inconsistency they are due to announce the Library. The prolongation is possible only before the loan time limit has gone.

Reservation making

In case a document is not available (it´s loaned by somebody else), user can make a reservation for it. In case he has active e-mail address in his library account user will be informed via e-mail when the document is available for him.

Documents reserved via electronic catalogue are to be checked out till the date specified in the users library account. Claims for depository are to be checked till the date arranged while asking for reservation. Fines for late checking of the reserved documents are stated in the Price List of fees and paid services.

Handing back documents

Documents are to be handed back in stated loan period or to be prolonged.

User is to hand back the document in the same state he has borrowed it. He is not allowed to borrow it to anybody else and he guarantees for documents till he hands it back to the Library (also in case of sending it ). For every damage the Library may claim certain compensation (see the Price List of fees and paid services).

User can hand back documents he borrowed from the Library via Bibliobox in the foyer of the Faculty building. Documents handed back via Bibliobox are cut out of a user’s library account the next week-day. In case of a running fine connected to late handing back of the document, the fine will stay not paid till the user will come to the Library to pay it. Documents loaned via inter-library service are to be handed back only during the visit of the Library.

Claiming of the overdue documents

In case of late handing, the Library claims a fine and may send a notice to the user. After the 4th notice there follows an exaction by the law. For all fines see the Price List of fees and paid Services.

Reimbursement of caused damages

User is due to announce every damage or loss of the document he borrowed and during certain time-limit to compensate the damage. The way of compensation specifies the Library head-master or an employer in charge of it.

Rules of Study Room of LFE

To use services of the Study Room user is to present a valid Card of Charles University and to sign into a user’s book at the Service Desk in the Study Room. In case he doesn´t have the CU card he pays a fee.

User of the Study Room is due to let his belongings on place intended for it during all his visit and follow orders of the service at the Service Desk. Mobile-phones are to be in silent mode.

All documents in Study Room are to be kept as in-house documents.

Every out-comes of e-sources are to use for personal usage only and there mustn´t be any violation of Copyright Act. Any abuse can mean legal steps against the user.

While leaving the Study Room user puts documents on their place, reference documents and documents borrowed as in-house he gives back to the Service Desk.

Full version of rules of Study Room are also available at the Service Desk.

Finall

In case of any violation of this Loan Rules, rights of the user can be blocked or cancelled.

Exceptions of this Loan Rules are allowed only by Head of the Library.
