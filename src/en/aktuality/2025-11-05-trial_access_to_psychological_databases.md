hide: "2025-12-02"

Until 02.12.2025, Charles University has a trial access to the following databases: 
the [APA PsycBooks](https://cuni.primo.exlibrisgroup.com/permalink/420CKIS_INST/gf08nd/alma9926125499906986) and 
[APA PsycTests](https://cuni.primo.exlibrisgroup.com/permalink/420CKIS_INST/gf08nd/alma9926125499806986) databases from the American Psychological
Association; they provide extensive scholarly resources in psychology.
PsycBooks includes over 4,600 titles, featuring reference works and classic
texts. PsycTests offers thousands of psychological assessments, measurement
tools, and surveys with detailed methodology and validity data.
