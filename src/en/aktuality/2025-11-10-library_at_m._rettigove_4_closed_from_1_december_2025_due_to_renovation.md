<p>We are expanding and improving our spaces for studying and meeting.</p>
<p>Please make sure to borrow all the books you will need in time – <strong>the last day for borrowing is Friday, 28 November 2025.</strong></p>

<h3>During the closure:</h3>
<ul>
<li>all loans due during this period will be <strong>automatically extended until 4 May 2026</strong>,</li>
<li>books can be <strong>returned at any time via the return box next to the porter’s lodge</strong>,</li>
<li><strong>borrowing (including through reservation boxes) will not be possible</strong>,</li>
<li>the <strong>study room in Celetná 13 will remain open</strong> as usual.</li>
</ul>

<p>For the latest updates, please follow the faculty website and social media.  
Thank you for your understanding – we look forward to welcoming you back in our renewed library.</p>
