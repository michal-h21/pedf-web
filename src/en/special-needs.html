Study room and services

Our study room is equiped by a computer with screen reader NVDA, Fine Reader and Office 2019 instaled, with reading and dicting functions. Users are allowed to use this computer alone or with assistance of the study room service.

There are 245 digitized books available for students with special needs. They are available from the websites of the Library. We are also able to digitize other textbooks on demand regarding to the special need of a student. Fulltexts are available with a password given after registration in our Library and confirmation of Declaration of a user. With this registration are handicapped users allowed also to print free of charge materials used for their studies.

Digitised documents are to find in our library catalogue and in portal of Masaryk University .

These documents are available for our handicapped students only from the list on websites of our Library .

Loan services for students on a rolling chair are poceeded in the study room.
