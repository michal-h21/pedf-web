Copying and Printing

In our study facilities, we offer the following reprographic services:

  • Self-service black-and-white and color copying and printing.
  • Free document scanning to a computer.

At the Rettigová Study Room, we also offer additional services:

  • Spiral binding
  • Laminating

Fees for using these services can be found in the price list. More information about the library rules can be found in the Library Rules and Regulations of the Faculty of Education, CU.

Any copies made should be for the user's personal use only and in accordance with the Copyright Act.
