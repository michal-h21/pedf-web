Biblioboxes

Biblioboxes are metal return boxes used for the safe and reliable return of books during faculty opening hours. You can find them at the following locations:

  • Magdalény Rettigové 4: In the faculty lobby, to the left of the reception desk.
  • Celetná 13: In front of the entrance to the Study Room Celetná.

Rules for Using Biblioboxes

  • Important: Please use the Bibliobox only for books borrowed from the specific library branch where the box is located. Books borrowed from other PedF branches or other libraries cannot be returned via the incorrect Bibliobox.
  • Insert books into the Bibliobox one by one, spine first.
  • Books returned via the Bibliobox will be removed from your reader account on the following working day.
  • Any outstanding overdue fines will remain on your reader account and must be paid in person during your next visit to the library.
  • Books that have been damaged in any way compared to their condition when borrowed will not be removed from the reader account.
  • You can verify your return transactions online via your reader account.
  • We recommend returning books borrowed through the Interlibrary Loan Service (ILL) only in person at the library.
  • Returning borrowed books is secure. The Bibliobox is designed to be resistant to unauthorized removal of books.
