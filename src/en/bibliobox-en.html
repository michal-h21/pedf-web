Rules for Using the Bibliobox

  • The Bibliobox allows for the safe and reliable return of books on workdays during the faculty's opening hours.
  • Please use the Bibliobox exclusively for books borrowed from the PedF Library in the main faculty building at Magdalény Rettigové 4.
  • Loans from other libraries cannot be returned here.
  • Please insert books into the Bibliobox one by one, spine first.
  • Books returned via the Bibliobox will be removed from your reader account on the following working day.
  • Any outstanding overdue fines will remain on your reader account and must be paid in person during your next visit to the library.
  • Books that have been damaged in any way compared to their condition when borrowed will not be removed from the reader account.
  • You can verify your return transactions online via your reader account.
  • We recommend returning books borrowed through the Interlibrary Loan Service only in person at the library.
  • Returning borrowed books is secure. The Bibliobox is designed to be resistant to unauthorized removal of books.
