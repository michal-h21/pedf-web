Study Room Celetná

To improve the study room services, we placed a box for returning books in front of the study room. You can return books here. Now that the library is closed, please write an e-mail about returning the books to the Bibliobox at knihovna@pedf.cuni.cz, so we can mark them as returned immediately.

Please, don´t put the books in a box in basement of Celetná 13, OUR box is in front of the studyroom :-)

How to get to Celetná no.13 by metro: Náměstí republiky station or Staroměstská station and after a few minutes by walk to Celetná street
