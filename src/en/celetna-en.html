Study Room Celetná

In June 2020 users are allowed lend and give back documents from collection Celetna. The studyroom is not open yet for studying.

In the loaning desk is open every Wednesday from 9am to 2:30pm in June and July.

The studyroom is closed in August.

Books have to be ordered in advance via the CU Central Catalogue. When you find a book in the catalogue, make sure it is located in FEdu Celetná. To make the request, choose the option Request on the left side of the web page. The value in the Due date column has to be On shelf. When the reservation is ready, we will send you a confirmation e-mail with further instructions.
