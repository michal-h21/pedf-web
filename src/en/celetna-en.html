Study Room Celetná

The Study Room offers textbooks, monographs, reference literature, and specialized databases for the study of English, German and Russian language and literature. A box for returning books is available in front of the Study Room.

In addition to the librarians present, the study room also has a self-service device (self-check). Thanks to it, you can borrow and return books independently. Simply place your student card or employee card to the reader and put the books in the designated place. You can also check your reader account via selfcheck.

Disabled users can ask for the service in the porter´s lodge to accompany them to the lift, and then to the 2nd floor. They will help you to use the deck system that will bring you to the study room on the 3rd floor. There is also a barrier-free toilette in the building.

How to get to Celetná no.13 by metro: Náměstí republiky station or Staroměstská station and after a few minutes by walk to Celetná street
