First Time in the Library

This brief guide will help you navigate our services and facilities during your first visit.

Essential Steps for Newcomers

  • Student/Staff ID Card: To use our services, you need a valid Charles University ID card with an activated library registration.
  • Registration: Registration is completed electronically by confirming the Terms of Use upon your first login to the UKAŽ discovery service.

Important Documents and Links

  • Guide for Freshmen – A complete summary of everything you need to know to get started.
  • Borrowing Rules – Information on loan categories, renewal policies, and late fees.
  • CU Library Rules – The official document defining your rights and obligations (PDF).
  • Library Layout and Premises – Where to find study rooms, computers, and book stacks.

Where to Find Us?

The library is located in the basement of the main Faculty of Education building (Magdalény Rettigové 4, Prague 1).

Tip: Use the central portal UKAŽ to search for books, renew your loans, and access electronic resources.
