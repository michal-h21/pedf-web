Smart lockers Test operation

You do not have time to visit the library during opening hours?

You can pick up your book order from a smart locker, located in front of Study Room at any time during the opening hours Faculty of Education M.Rettigové 4.

How to do it?

Just make a reservation for a book in the Discovery Service. We can place your order in the lockers only if you have Czech phone number available in your Library account, and if there are any free lockers available.

As soon as the books in the smart lockers will be ready for pick up, we will send you a text message with the code. Then you will enter the code with your phone number on the touch screen of the smart locker.

The capacity of smart lockers is limited. If your order does not fit in, we will keep the books in the Reference Desk and you will receive a confirmation e-mail.

If you do not know what to do, you can write mail to knihovna@pedf.cuni.cz or call 221 900 148
